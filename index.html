About Montheri Trading

Driving sustainable growth across Africa's resource sector

Montheri Trading (Pty) Ltd is a 100% black-owned South African company established in 2015, operating across the mining, commodity trading, and geological services sectors.


Our purpose is to drive growth and empowerment within Africa's mineral and energy industries through strategic partnerships, innovation, and operational excellence.


We are expanding into regional markets including Botswana, Zimbabwe, Zambia, Malawi, Gabon, and the DRC, with a vision to build Montheri Holdings as the parent company for our future divisions ‚Äî including Energy and Fuel Commodities.

SAIL Group - Black Chrome Mine

2018 - Present | Limpopo

Scope: Sub-contract mining & heavy machinery leasing

Reserve: 20,000,000 tonnes with LG6A and LG6B chromite seams

Equipment: Two 90-tonne excavators, four 45-tonne dump trucks, one dozer (D6R), and an 18,000 litre water cart

Fulfilled contract demands with minimal environmental impact and wildlife movement considerations.

Annesley Andalusite Mine

Recent | Burgersfort, Limpopo

Scope: 1-year plant hire contract

Equipment: Cat D6R2 dozer

Output Support: 30,000 tonnes of Andalusite per month

Maintaining commitment to source the best machinery, giving clients confidence to successfully reach their targets.

Natal Portland Cement

2016-2019 | KwaZulu Natal

Scope: Contract mining project

Output: 10,000 tonnes of de-dolomitised limestone monthly

Contractor: Worked alongside Unitrans (main contractor)
