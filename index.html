About Montheri Trading

Our Story

The story of Montheri Trading began in 2015 when it was registered with CIPC. It is no surprise that those years have been used optimally to surpass expectations and still aim higher with the passing of every milestone.

Montheri Trading has, for the past 5 years, utilised over 100 years of industry experience by bringing in notable industry pacesetters to partner up and provide end-to-end solutions to challenges facing the customer.

Our Directors' Expertise

Our Directors have a string of successful projects under their belts in the industries we operate in and it is this wealth of knowledge that we tap into to not only see where we are going but also forecast where we need to position ourselves for maximum ascendancy for the benefit of our clients, employees and the industry we serve in.

Our Business Focus

Our interests in commodity trading put us right in the front line of extracting commodities, moving goods and ensuring productivity in mining and road transportation. This is a business that introduces us to the vast opportunities that exist in these economy-bench-marking activities.

Contract Mining - Completed

Natal Portland Cement - Limestone Extraction

Client: Natal Portland Cement (PPC)

Location: KwaZulu-Natal

Duration: 2016 - 2019 (3 Years)

Main Contractor: Unitrans

Production: 10,000 tonnes/month

Material: De-dolomitised Limestone

Project Significance

Our first project and why we hold it dear to our hearts and as an integral part of our history is the Contract Mining project for Natal Portland Cement in KwaZulu Natal. This project established our reputation in the industry.

Technical Challenges

In this project we were tasked with extracting 10,000 tonnes of de-dolomitised limestone monthly. We were working alongside the main contractor, Unitrans, where we were tasked with extracting this metamorphic rock which meant we had to work around the challenges that come with such rocks in the form of fault lines and imperfections in the formation.

Key Achievements

  • Successfully completed 3-year contract with zero safety incidents
  • Consistently met monthly production targets of 10,000 tonnes
  • Overcame complex geological challenges with metamorphic rock formations
  • Managed fault lines and formation imperfections effectively
  • Established strong working relationship with Unitrans
  • Built foundation for future contract mining opportunities
"This project was the foundation of our reputation - delivering excellence in challenging geological conditions from 2016 to 2019"
